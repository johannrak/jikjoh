BERRY, MINT & ALMOND CHIA BOWL

  • 8.5g|20g(net carbs|total)
  • 6.9g protein
  • 19g|37%(total fat|sat.)

est.nutritional values per one serving, may vary depending on ingredients used.

INGREDIENTS
  1. 2 tbsps chia seeds(~ 22g)
  2. 2 tbsps shredded coconut,unsweetened(~15g)
  3. 1/3 cup almond milk, unsweetened(~40g)
  4. 2 med strawberries, washed & quartered(~40g)
  5. 8-10 blueberried,washed(~20g)
  6. 1 tbsp sliced alomds
  7. 2-3 mint leaves,torn
DIRECTIONS
  1. Mix the chia seeds,coconut and almond milk in a bowl. Set aside for 10 mins
  2. Add the berries, almonds & mint leaves.

    3. ENJOY!

    You can subsitute some of the berried with e.g raspberries or add them to the mix
